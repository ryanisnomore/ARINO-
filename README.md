## About ARINO
ARINO is an advanced Discord music bot designed to deliver seamless, high-quality audio streaming to your server 24/7. With multi-platform support and robust features, ARINO enhances your server's audio experience with reliability and ease of use.

## Key Features 
- **Multi-Platform Support**:
  - Deezer
  - Spotify
  - SoundCloud
  - Apple Music
  - YouTube
- **High-Quality Audio Streaming**
- **24/7 Uptime & Reliability**
- **Easy-to-Use Commands**
- **Queue Management System**
- **Playlist Support**
- **Low Latency Performance**

## Quick Setup
1. Invite ARINO to your server: [Invite Link](https://dsc.gg/arino)
2. Use `+play [song/playlist]` to start music
3. Control playback with intuitive commands
